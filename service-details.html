Thesis: E-Singnage Automatic Voice Notification System

This project focuses on the design and development of an innovative E-Signage system integrated with automatic voice notifications, aimed at enhancing communication in busy university environments. Implemented on the 3rd floor of Laguna University's New Building, the system utilizes an Arduino microcontroller and an ESP8266 WIFI module to remotely manage announcements via an Android smartphone app. The system also addresses classroom distractions by detecting noise levels and providing timely voice alerts. The project's effectiveness is evaluated using ISO/IEC 25010:2011 standards, ensuring a comprehensive assessment of its performance, reliability, and usability.
